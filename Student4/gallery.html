G A L L E R Y

INVENTOR

WILLIAM.G.MORGAN

Volleyball was invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan, physical director of the Young Men's Christian Association (YMCA) in Holyoke, Massachusetts. It was designed as an indoor sport for businessmen who found the new game of basketball too vigorous.

Volley BALL

The standard volleyball specifications

FIVB regulations state that the ball must be spherical, made of leather or synthetic leather, have a circumference of 65-67 cm (26-26 in), a weight of 260-280 g (9.2-9.9 oz) and an interior air pressure of 0.30-0.325 kg/cm2

National Sport

Volleyball in Sri Lanka

Sri lanka has a proud history of adapting volleyball as sport. In 1916 volleyball was initiated in srilanka as a sport and was selected as the national sport thereafter.

Guiness World Record

Most passes in a single game

The record for most consecutive passes in volleyball is 110, achieved in the United States, in Raleigh, North Carolina, on February 5, 2010.

World Champs

Volleyball as a sport is famous for both men and women where both genders are capable of producing miracles in their own way. The 2022 women's world champions serbia beat no 1 nation for volleyball Brazil to win the world cup in a spectaculer way.
